About Product

Q-Cards enables you to share any record from your phonebook with someone else. It also allows you to selectthe data that you want to share. For exampleyou may share your work contact detail data but not personal contact details.As the share/sender, you generate the code

The receiver scan the QR using the app and adds the data to his phonebook. Otherwise the receiver uses any QR Code scanner and click to go to the URL contained in the QR code and adds the data to their phonebook
